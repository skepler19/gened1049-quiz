You got Wong Kar-Wai! WKW is a one-of-a-kind auteur who has developed a global audience for his compelling and visually stunning films. He is known for using vibrant colors, such as red and yellow, to fill the frame with an intensity that is also reflected in the music he uses. One unique aspect of Wong Kar-Wai cinematographic style is his use of step printing, which creates the illusion of a single or few people moving in slow motion while everything is moving quickly around them. In this way, WKW captures the hustle and bustle of Hong Kong, while forcing his audience to focus on the scenes and details of his choosing.
