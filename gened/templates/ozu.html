You got Yasujiro Ozu! Ozu is known for breaking out of traditional cinematographic norms, creating an experience unlike any other. His work is reflective and elliptical, with several similar themes showing up throughout and across his works. Ozu has been described as a “master of simplicity,” finding depth in the mundane and meaning within routine. Ozu’s cinematographic techniques are unlike any other director’s, and you’re sure to find a comfort within his works of art.
